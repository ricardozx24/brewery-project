A Better Leader

What are the outcomes of strong leadership?

Integrity, approachability, communication and transparency.

Growth among others, Goals being achieved and good morale among team.

Employees enjoy coming to work. They learn to work together and become one team to reach the daily goal. They feel comfortable coming to a manager when they have issues.

Earning the Respect from you team.

Great results and continuous improvement, as well as employee engagement and development.

Success, teamwork, and an environment where everyone enjoys being here.

A strong Leadership team can provide motivation for his or her team. It’s hard to do but create a positive environment. Provide the right training and you will increase production and Accuracy, when everyone is on the same page.

What are important habits a leader should have?

Being consistent and follow-through. Often some of the frustration AutoZoners deal with are due to inconsistencies or feeling that their issue was just swept under the rug.

As a leader it’s important to make it a habit to be consistent across the board even if this means not everyone is content. We need to ensure to address and update an AutoZoner whether they are still researching the issue brough to their attention or provide a response.

Patience, Empathy, Accountability, Fairness and Consistent, Optimistic and the ability to make good business decisions. Team player, Integrity and Initiative.

Time management, organization, deliberate development, game planning and game filming.

Patience. ** Not everyone is the same.** Listen and understand what workers are asking you.
