A Better Leader

What is needed to become a better leader?

Knowing when someone needs you to listen and when to give advice.

Something needed to become a better leader is to keep an open mind and be receptive to criticism. The only way to continue to grow and learn from peers and people in higher leadership roles is to be open to being critiqued. This only helps develop you and should not be taken personal, it’s part of growth.

Be open-minded but also be strong enough to discipline.

Turning on your listening skills, let them know you care what they have to say.

Empathy, self-awareness and great communication.

Be open minded, driven, and want to make a difference.

Curiosity, Structure, Emotional Intelligence, Willingness to change, Open or even a seeker of feedback, listen to your people's concerns.

Which leadership gurus have had the biggest influence on you and why?

Linda Stewart - she gets the job done, she is hard core, almost like a military style, she has a great sense of humor.

My dad, my dad was a school dropout but owned his own Plumbing company with around 50 employees. He taught me that anything was achievable in life no matter what was in your past.

Bruce Loflin - he was caring but firm and was very open in communication.

Bruce Loflin – he is a leader that acknowledges talents and gives the opportunity to grow. Honest about in what areas you need to grow.

Not a big believer in gurus I do enjoy books on leadership – 7 Habits of highly effective people was a good read. It emphasizes the power of synergy, which I’m a high believer of.
